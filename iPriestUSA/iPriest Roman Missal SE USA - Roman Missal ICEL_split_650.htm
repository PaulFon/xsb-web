Option 2

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Ps 64 (63): 11

The just will rejoice in the Lord and hope in him, and all upright hearts will be praised (alleluia).

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who see that in our weakness we fail,
mercifully restore us to your love by the example of your Saints.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Grant us, we pray, almighty God,
that the humble offering we make
may be pleasing to you in honour of your Saints
and may purify us both in body and in mind.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of Saints I or Preface of Saints II

Communion Antiphon | Cf. Jn 12: 26

Whoever serves me must follow me, and where I am, there also will my servant be (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

On the heavenly birthday of your Saints,
we pray, O Lord, that we,
who are nourished by the gift of this Sacrament,
may delight for all eternity
in the good things with which even now
by your grace you nurture us.
Through Christ our Lord.
