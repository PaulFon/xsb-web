21. FOR THE NATION OR STATE

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who arrange all things
according to a wonderful design,
graciously receive the prayers
we pour out to you for our country (state),
that through the wisdom of its leaders
and the integrity of its citizens
harmony and justice may be assured and lasting prosperity come with peace.
Through our Lord.
