May 14
SAINT MATTHIAS, APOSTLE
Feast

Entrance Antiphon | Jn 15: 16

It was not you who chose me, says the Lord, but I who chose you and appointed you to go and bear fruit, fruit that will last (alleluia).

>> The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God) is said.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who assigned Saint Matthias
a place in the college of Apostles,
grant us, through his intercession,
that, rejoicing at how your love has been allotted to us,
we may merit to be numbered among the elect.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive, O Lord, the offerings of your Church,
reverently presented for the Feast of Saint Matthias,
and through them strengthen us by the power of your grace.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface I of the Apostles or Preface II.

Communion Antiphon | Jn 15: 12

This is my commandment: love one another as I love you, says the Lord (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

Never cease, O Lord, we pray,
to fill your family with divine gifts,
and, through blessed Matthiasâ€™ intercession for us,
graciously admit us to a share in the lot of the Saints in light.

Through Christ our Lord.

The Solemn Blessing formula may be used.
