January 22*
Day of Prayer for the Legal Protection of Unborn Children
[In the Dioceses of the United States]
(*January 23, when January 22 falls on a Sunday)

From the Masses and Prayers for Various Needs and Occasions: For Giving Thanks to God for the Gift of Human Life (no. 48/1), with white vestments; or
For the Preservation of Peace and Justice (no. 30), with violet vestments.
