B. For another Bishop

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who chose your servant,
the Bishop (Cardinal), N.,
from among your Priests.
and endowed him with pontifical dignity
in the apostolic priesthood,
grant, we pray, that he may also be admitted
to their company for ever.

Through our Lord.

Prayer over the Offerings

Accept, O Lord, we pray,
the sacrificial gifts we offer
for the soul of your servant, the Bishop (Cardinal), N.,
that as you accorded him
the pontifical dignity in this world,
so you may command him to be admitted
to the company of your Saints in the heavenly Kingdom.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Prayer after Communion

We pray, almighty and merciful God,
that as you made your servant, the Bishop (Cardinal), N.,
an ambassador for Christ on earth,
so you may raise him, purified by this sacrifice,
to be seated with Christ in heaven.

Who lives and reigns for ever and ever.
