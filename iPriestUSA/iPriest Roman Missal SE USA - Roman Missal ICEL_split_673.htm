4. FOR THE ELECTION OF A POPE OR A BISHOP

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. 1 Sam 2:35

I will raise up for myself a faithful priest who shall do according to my heart and mind. I will establish a lasting house for him and he shall walk before me all his days.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, eternal shepherd,
who govern your flock with unfailing care,
grant in your boundless fatherly love
a pastor for your Church
who will please you by his holiness
and for us show watchful concern.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

May your abundant kindness favour us, O Lord,
that through the sacred offerings we reverently bring to you,
we may come to rejoice that a pastor pleasing to your majesty
presides over your holy Church.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface: The unity of the Body of Christ, which is the Church.

Communion Antiphon | Jn 15.16

I chose you and appointed you to go and bear fruit that will remain, says the Lord.

Prayer after Communion

As we have been renewed, O Lord,
with the supreme Sacrament of salvation,
the Body and Blood of your Only Begotten Son,
may the wondrous grace of your majesty
gladden us with the gift of a shepherd
who will instruct your people by his virtues
and imbue the minds of the faithful
with the truth of the Gospel.
Through Christ our Lord.
