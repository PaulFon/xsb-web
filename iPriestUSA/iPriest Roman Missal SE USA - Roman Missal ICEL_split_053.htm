Christmas time (after Epiphany) | Monday

Entrance Antiphon

A holy day has dawned upon us: Come, you nations, and adore the Lord, for a great light has come down upon the earth.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, whose eternal Word adorns the face of the heavens
yet accepted from the Virgin Mary
the frailty of our flesh, grant, we pray, that he who appeared among us as the splendour of truth
may go forth in the fullness of power
for the redemption of the world.

Who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive our oblation, O Lord,
by which is brought about a glorious exchange,
that, by offering what you have given,
we may merit to receive your very self.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of the Epiphany or of the Nativity: Preface I, Preface II, or Preface III.

Communion Antiphon | Jn 1: 14

We have seen his glory, the glory of an only Son coming from the Father, filled with grace and truth.

Prayer after Communion

Grant, we pray, almighty God,
that, by the power of these holy mysteries,
our life may be constantly sustained.

Through Christ our Lord.
