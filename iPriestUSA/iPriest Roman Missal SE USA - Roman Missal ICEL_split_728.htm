39. FOR CHASTITY

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Purify our hearts, O Lord,
by the heavenly fire of the Holy Spirit,
that we may serve you with a chaste body and please you with a pure heart.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

May our gifts be acceptable to you, O Lord,
that by your gift
we may offer you the sacrifice of praise in full freedom
and with a pure mind, for you have been pleased
to save us by your grace.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface: Common I | C II | C III | C IV | C V | C VI

Prayer after Communion

Through the Sacraments we have received
may our heart and our body flourish anew
by a keen sense of modesty and renewed chastity,
so that what has passed our lips as food
we may possess in purity of heart.
Through Christ our Lord.
