A. Outside Easter Time

Entrance Antiphon | Rev 21:4

God will wipe every tear from their eyes, and there shall be no more death or mourning, crying or pain, for former things have passed away.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, glory of the faithful and life of the just,
by the Death and Resurrection of whose Son
we have been redeemed,
look mercifully on your departed servant N.,
that just as he (she) professed
the mystery of our resurrection
he (she) may merit to receive
the joys of eternal happiness.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look with favour, we pray, O Lord,
on the offerings we make
for the soul of your servant N.,
that, being cleansed by heavenly remedies,
his (her) soul may be ever alive and blessed in your glory.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Communion Antiphon Cf. Jn 11:25; 3:36; 5:24

I am the Resurrection and the Life, says the Lord. Whoever believes in me has eternal life and does not come to judgment, but will pass from death to life.

Prayer after Communion

Restored by these sacred mysteries,
we humbly beseech you, O Lord,
that your servant N. may be cleansed
from all offenses and merit for all eternity
the precious gift of the resurrection.

Through Christ our Lord.
