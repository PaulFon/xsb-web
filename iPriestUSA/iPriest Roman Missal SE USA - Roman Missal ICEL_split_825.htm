>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

To you, O Lord, we commend your servants (N. and N.)
that, having died to this world,
they may live for you,
and we beseech you
that whatever sins they committed
through the frailty of the flesh
during their earthly lives
you may in your most tender mercy
pardon and wipe away.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Be merciful, we pray, O Lord,
to your servants (N. and N.),
for whom we offer you
the sacrifice of conciliation,
and since in this life they remained faithful to you,
may they have their reward
in your presence.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Prayer after Communion

Grant, we pray, almighty God,
that, through the power of this Sacrament,
your departed servants
be made partakers of eternal happiness
in the gathering of the just.

Through Christ our Lord.
