Christmas time (after Epiphany) | Friday

Entrance Antiphon | Ps 112 (111): 4

A light has risen in the darkness for the upright of heart; the Lord is generous, merciful and just.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Grant, we ask, almighty God,
that the Nativity of the Saviour of the world,
made known by the guidance of a star,
may be revealed ever more fully to our minds.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive with favour, O Lord, we pray,
the offerings of your people, that what they profess with devotion and faith
may be theirs through these heavenly mysteries.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of the Epiphany or of the Nativity: Preface I, Preface II, or Preface III.

Communion Antiphon | 1 Jn 4: 9

By this the love of God was revealed to us: God sent his Only Begotten Son into the world, so that we might have life through him.

Prayer after Communion

O God, who touch us
through our partaking of your Sacrament,
work, we pray, the effects of its power in our hearts,
that we may be made fit to receive your gift
through this very gift itself.

Through Christ our Lord.
