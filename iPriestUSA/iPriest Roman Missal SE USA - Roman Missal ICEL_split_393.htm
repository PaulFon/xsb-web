March 8
Saint John of God, Religious

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who filled Saint John of God
with a spirit of compassion,
grant, we pray, that, giving ourselves to works of charity,
we may merit to be found among the elect in your Kingdom.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of Holy Men and Women: For Religious, or Holy Men and Women: For Those Who Practiced Works of Mercy.
