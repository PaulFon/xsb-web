40. FOR CHARITY

Entrance Antiphon

I will take from your bodies your stony hearts and giving you natural hearts. I will put my spirit within you. You shall be my people, and I will be your God.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Set our hearts aflame, O Lord,
with the Spirit of your charity,
we pray, that we may always think thoughts
worthy and pleasing to your majesty
and love you sincerely in our brothers and sisters.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Sanctify these gifts in your mercy, O Lord,
and accepting the oblation of the spiritual sacrifice,
grant, we pray, that we may extend your charity to all.
Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer for Reconciliation I or for Reconciliation II

Communion Antiphon

So faith, hope, love remain, these three; but the greatest of these is love.

Prayer after Communion

Pour out the grace of the Holy Spirit,
O Lord, on those you have replenished with the one Bread of heaven,
and refresh us, we pray,
with the delights of perfect charity.
Through Christ our Lord.
