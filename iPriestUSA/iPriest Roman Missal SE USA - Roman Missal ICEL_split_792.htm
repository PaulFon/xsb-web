14. ALL THE HOLY APOSTLES

In this Mass, the colour red is used.

Entrance Antiphon | Jn 15:16

It was not you who chose me, says the Lord, but I who chose you and appointed you to go and bear fruit, fruit that will remain.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

May your Church always exult, O Lord,
in the honour given through the ages
to the blessed Apostles,
that she may be guided by these protectors
in whose teaching and merits she finds joy.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Pour out on us, Lord, your Holy Spirit,
whom you poured in abundance upon the Apostles,
that we may recognise
what you have given us through them,
and duly offer the sacrifice of praise to your glory.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface I of the Apostles or Preface II.

Communion Antiphon | Mt 19:28

You who have followed me will sit on twelve thrones, judging the twelve tribes of Israel, says the Lord.

Prayer after Communion

Grant that we may persevere, O God,
with rejoicing and simplicity of heart in the teaching of the Apostles,
in the breaking of the bread,
and in the prayers.

Through Christ our Lord,
