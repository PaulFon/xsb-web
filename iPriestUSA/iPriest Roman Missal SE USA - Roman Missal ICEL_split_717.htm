>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Lord, to whom no one is a stranger,
and from whose help no one is ever distant,
look with compassion on refugees and exiles,
on segregated persons and on lost children; restore them,
we pray, to a homeland, and give us a kind heart
for the needy and for strangers.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Lord, who willed that your Son should lay down his life
to gather into one your scattered children,
grant that this sacrifice of your peace
may bring about a communion of minds and hearts
and an increase in fraternal charity.
Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: IV. Jesus, who went about doing good.

Communion Antiphon | Ps 90:2

My refuge and my stronghold, my God, in whom I trust.

Prayer after Communion

Lord, who have renewed us
with the one Bread and the one Chalice,
grant that in sincerity of heart
we may show true compassion towards strangers and the abandoned, and that all of us may deserve to be gathered
together at last in the land of the living.
Through Christ our Lord.
