August 13
Saints Pontian, Pope, and Hippolytus, Priest, Martyrs

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

May the precious long-suffering of the just,
O Lord, we pray,
bring us a great increase of love for you
and always prompt in our hearts
constancy in the holy faith.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of Martyrs: For Several Martyrs, or Common of Pastors: For Several Pastors.
