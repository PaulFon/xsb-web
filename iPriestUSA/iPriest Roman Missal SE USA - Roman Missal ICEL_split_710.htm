>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Pour out your gracious blessing on your people,
Lord God,
so that through your generosity
our land may yield its fruits
for us to enjoy with ever-grateful hearts
to the honour of your holy Name.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look with favour on our offerings, O Lord,
so that we, who bring you grains of wheat made into bread
to be changed into the Body of your Son,
may find joy in the blessing you bestow
on the seed to be sown in the earth.
Through Christ our Lord.
