38. FOR THE FORGIVENESS OF SINS

Option A

Entrance Antiphon

You have mercy on all, O Lord, and hate nothing that you have made.  You disregard people’s sins to lead them to repentance and you spare them, for you are the Lord our God. | Cf. Wis 11:24-25, 27

> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Graciously hear the prayers of those who call upon you,
we ask, O Lord,
and forgive the sins of those who confess to you,
granting us in your kindness both pardon and peace.
Through our Lord.

Or:

Be merciful to your people, O Lord,
and absolve them from all sins, so that what we deserve by our offenses may be avoided by your pardon.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

We offer you, O Lord,
the sacrifice of conciliation and praise that,
being moved to compassion,
you may both absolve our offenses and direct our wavering hearts.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface IV of the Sundays in Ordinary Time, or
Eucharistic Prayer for Reconciliation I or for Reconciliation II

Communion Antiphon

There will be rejoicing among the angels of God over one sinner who repents. Lk 15:10

Prayer after Communion

Grant us, merciful God,
that, receiving in this gift the forgiveness of sins,
we may be able by your grace to avoid sinning from now on
and to serve you in sincerity of heart.
Through Christ our Lord.
