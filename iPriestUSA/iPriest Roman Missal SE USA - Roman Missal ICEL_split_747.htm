III. FOR THE ADMINISTERING OF VIATICUM.

This Mass may be used with the colour white on days when Ritual Masses are permitted.

All the prayers given for a man may be adapted for a woman, with the necessary change of gender; in addition, those expressed in the plural form may be used for individuals, with the necessary change to the singular.

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Ps 80:17

He fed them with the finest wheat, and satisfied them with honey from the rock (alleluia).

or: | Cf. Is 53:4

Truly the Lord has borne our infirmities, and he has carried our sufferings (alleluia).

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, whose Son is for us the way, the truth and the life,
look lovingly upon your servant N.
and grant that, trusting in your promises
and strengthened by the Body of your Son,
he (she) may journey in peace to your Kingdom.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look graciously on our sacrifice, holy Father,
that it may present to you the Paschal Lamb
whose Passion has unlocked the gates of paradise,
and by your grace lead your servant N.
to the gift of eternal life.

Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: IV. Jesus, who went about doing good.

Communion Antiphon | Jn 6:54

Whoever eats my flesh and drinks my blood has eternal life, says the Lord, and I will raise him on the last day (alleluia).

or: | Col 1:24

In my flesh I am completing what is lacking in the afflictions of Christ on behalf of his body, which is the Church (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

O Lord, eternal health and salvation
of those who believe in you,
grant, we pray, that your servant N.,
renewed by heavenly food and drink,
may safely reach your Kingdom of light and life.

Through Christ our Lord.
