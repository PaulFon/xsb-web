43. FOR THOSE HELD IN CAPTIVITY

Entrance Antiphon

O Lord and God of my salvation, I have cried before you day and night.  Let my prayer come into your presence. Incline your ear to my cry.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, whose Son humbled himself
and took the form of a slave
to redeem the human race from being captive to sin,
grant to your servants held in confinement
that they may obtain the freedom you destine to all,
for we are your sons and daughters.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Through the saving Sacrament of human redemption
which we offer you, Lord,
grant that your servants may be released from captivity
and rejoice in lasting freedom of their soul.
Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer IV

Communion Antiphon

I will praise God’s name with a song, and I will glorify him with thanksgiving.  For the Lord listens to the needy, and does not spurn his own in their chains.

Prayer after Communion

Mindful of the price paid for our deliverance,
we implore your mercy, O Lord,
upon our brothers and sisters,
that they may be released from captivity and become servants of your justice.
Through Christ our Lord.
