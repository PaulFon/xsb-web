August 5
The Dedication of the Basilica of Saint Mary Major

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Pardon the faults of your servants, we pray, O Lord,
that we, who cannot please you by our own deeds,
may be saved through the intercession
of the Mother of your Son and our Lord.

Who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of the Blessed Virgin Mary.
