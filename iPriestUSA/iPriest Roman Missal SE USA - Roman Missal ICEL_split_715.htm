B - Other prayers for peace

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, Creator of the world,
under whose governance
the design for all the ages unfolds,
be attentive, we pray, to our petitions,
and grant to our times tranquility and peace,
that we may exult with unceasing joy
in praise of your great mercy.
Through our Lord.

Or:

O God of peace,
who are peace itself,
and whom a spirit of discord cannot grasp,
nor a violent mind receive,
grant that those who are one in heart may persevere in what is good,
and that those in conflict
may forget evil and so be healed.
Through our Lord.

The Mass for Promoting Harmony may also be used.
