Option D

Entrance Antiphon

Father, I pray for those who will believe in me, that they may be one in us, so that the world may believe it was you who sent me. Cf. Jn 17:20-21

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Almighty ever-living God,
who in Christ revealed your glory to all the nations,
watch over the works of your mercy
that holy Church,
spread throughout the whole world,
may persevere with steadfast faith in confessing your Name.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

O God, who constantly sanctify your Church
through the same sacrifice
by which you have made her clean,
grant that, united to Christ her Head,
she may offer herself to you with him,
and be united with you in purity of will.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface VIII of the Sundays in Ordinary Time, or
Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: I. The Church on the Path of Unity

Communion Antiphon

Whoever remains in me and I in him will bear much fruit, because without me you can do nothing, says the Lord.

Prayer after Communion

Be pleased, O Lord, we pray,
to govern the Church
you have nourished by this holy meal,
so that, firmly directed,
she may grow in freedom
and persevere in integrity of religion.
Through Christ our Lord.
