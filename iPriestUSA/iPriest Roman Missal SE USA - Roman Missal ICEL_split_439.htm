June 3
Saint Charles Lwanga and Companions, Martyrs
Memorial

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Wis 3: 6-7, 9

As gold in the furnace, the Lord put his chosen to the test; as sacrificial offerings, he took them to himself; and in due time they will be honoured, and grace and peace will be with the elect of God (alleluia).

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who have made the blood of Martyrs
the seed of Christians,
mercifully grant that the field which is your Church,
watered by the blood shed by Saints Charles Lwanga and his companions, may be fertile and always yield you an abundant harvest.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

We offer you sacrifice, O Lord, humbly praying that,
as you granted the blessed Martyrs
grace to die rather than sin, so you may bring us to minister at your altar in dedication to you alone.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface I of Holy Martyrs or Preface II

Communion Antiphon | Ps 116 (115): 15

How precious in the eyes of the Lord is the death of his holy ones. (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

We have received this divine Sacrament, O Lord,
as we celebrate the victory of your holy Martyrs;
may what helped them to endure torment,
we pray, make us, in the face of trials,
steadfast in faith and in charity.

Through Christ our Lord.
