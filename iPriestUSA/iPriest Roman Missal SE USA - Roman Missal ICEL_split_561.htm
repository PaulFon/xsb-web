November 13
Saint Frances Xavier Cabrini, Virgin
Memorial
[In the Dioceses of the United States]

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

God our Father,
who called Saint Frances Xavier Cabrini from Italy
to serve the immigrants of America,
by her example,
teach us to have concern for the stranger,
the sick, and all those in need,
and by her prayers help us to see Christ
in all the men and women we meet.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of Virgins: For One Virgin, or Common of Holy Men and Women: For Those Who Practiced Works of Mercy.
