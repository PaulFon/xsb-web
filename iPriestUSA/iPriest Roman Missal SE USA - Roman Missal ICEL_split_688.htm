>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O Lord, faithful God, grant, we pray,
that we may give you thanks
for your kindness towards our brother (sister) N.,
who today is eager to renew the gift received from you;
strengthen in him (her) a spirit of perfect charity,
so that each day
he (she) may more fervently serve your glory
and the work of your salvation.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive with our oblation,
O Lord,
the offering of himself (herself) which our brother (sister) N.
desires to reaffirm today,
and by the power of the Holy Spirit
graciously conform him (her) more fully
to the image of your beloved Son.
Who lives and reigns for ever and ever.

Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: II. God guides His Church along the Way of Salvation.

Communion Antiphon | Ps 41:2

Like the deer that yearns for running streams, so my soul is yearning for you, O God.

Prayer after Communion

We have partaken, O Lord,
of the Body and Blood of your Son,
which you have given us
on this joyful anniversary;
grant, we pray, that our brother (sister) N.,
refreshed with heavenly food and drink,
may proceed happily
on the journey towards you
already long begun.
Through Christ our Lord.
