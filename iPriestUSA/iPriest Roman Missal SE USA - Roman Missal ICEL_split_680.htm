9. FOR VOCATIONS TO HOLY ORDERS

Entrance Antiphon

Ask the Lord of the harvest to send out laborers for his harvest, says Jesus to his disciples.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God,
who willed to provide shepherds for your people,
pour out in your Church a spirit of piety and fortitude,
to raise up worthy ministers for your altars
and make them ardent yet gentle heralds of your Gospel.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look kindly, we pray, O Lord,
on the prayers and offerings of your people,
that the stewards of your mysteries may grow in number
and persevere always in your love.
Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: II. God guides His Church along the Way of Salvation.

Communion Antiphon

By this we came to know love that he laid down his life for us; so we ought to lay down our lives for our brothers.

Prayer after Communion

Renewed with the Bread of the heavenly table,
we entreat you, O Lord,
that through this Sacrament of charity,
the seeds you sow with great abundance in the field of your Church
may come to maturity,
so that many may make it their choice in life to serve you
in their brothers and sisters.
Through Christ our Lord.
