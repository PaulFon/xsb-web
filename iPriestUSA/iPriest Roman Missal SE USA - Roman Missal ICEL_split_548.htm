October 20
Saint Paul of the Cross, Priest
[In the Dioceses of the United States]

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. 1 Cor 2: 2

I resolved to know nothing while I was with you except for Jesus Christ, and him crucified.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

May the Priest Saint Paul,
whose only love was the Cross,
obtain for us your grace, O Lord,
so that, urged on more strongly by his example,
we may each embrace our own cross with courage.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Preface of Holy Pastors, or Preface of Saints I or Preface of Saints II

Communion Antiphon | 1 Cor 1: 23, 24

We proclaim Christ crucified; Christ, the power of God and the wisdom of God.

Prayer after Communion

O God, who in Saint Paul
have wonderfully made known the mystery of the Cross,
graciously grant that, drawing strength from this sacrifice,
we may cling faithfully to Christ
and labor in the Church for the salvation of all.

Through Christ our Lord.
