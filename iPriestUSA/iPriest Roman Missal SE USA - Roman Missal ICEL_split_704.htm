23. FOR A GOVERNING ASSEMBLY

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God,
who arrange all things in wondrous order and govern in marvelous ways,
look with favour on the assembled,
and mercifully pour out upon them
the spirit of your wisdom,
that they may decide everything
for the well-being and peace of all
and may never turn aside from your will.
Through our Lord.
