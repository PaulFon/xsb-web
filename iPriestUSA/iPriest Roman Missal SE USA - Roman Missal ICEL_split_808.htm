B. Outside Easter Time

Entrance Antiphon

Lord Jesus, grant eternal rest to him (her) for whom you poured out your precious Blood.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Send down, we pray, O Lord,
the lasting dew of your mercy
on your servant N.,
whose anniversary we commemorate,
and be pleased to grant him (her)
the company of your Saints.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Be attentive, O Lord,
to our prayers for your servant N.,
whose anniversary we observe today,
and through this sacrifice of conciliation and praise
graciously add him (her)
to the company of your Saints.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Communion Antiphon

You are rest after toil, O Lord, life after death: grant him (her) everlasting life.

Prayer after Communion

Just as you have lovingly accepted, O Lord,
our prayers and sacrificial offerings
for the soul of your servant N.,
so, we humbly entreat you,
if any stain of sin has clung to him (her) may it be wiped away
by your merciful forgiveness.

Through Christ our Lord.
