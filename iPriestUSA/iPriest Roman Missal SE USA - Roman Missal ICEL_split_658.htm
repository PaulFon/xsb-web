II. For Monks and Religious: D. For Religious
Option 1 or Option 2

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Ps 16 (15): 5

O Lord, it is you who are my portion and cup; you yourself who secure my portion (alleluia).

Or, for a Woman Religious: Cf. Hos 2: 21-22

The Lord has taken her as his bride for ever in faithfulness and mercy (alleluia).

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, by whose gift blessed N.
persevered in imitating Christ, poor and lowly,
grant us through his (her) intercession
that, faithfully walking in our own vocation,
we may reach the perfection
you have set before us in your Son.
Who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Most merciful God,
who were pleased to create in blessed N.
the New Man in your image, the old having passed away,
graciously grant, we pray, that, renewed like him (her),
we may offer you the acceptable sacrifice of conciliation.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of Saints I or Preface of Saints II

Communion Antiphon | Cf. Mt 19: 27-29

Amen I say to you, that you who have left all and followed me, will receive a hundredfold and possess eternal life (alleluia).

Or, for a woman Religious: Cf. Lam 3: 24-25

The Lord is my portion: he is good to the soul that seeks him (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

By the power of this Sacrament, Lord, we pray,
lead us always in your love,
through the example of blessed N.,
and bring to fulfillment the good work you have begun in us
until the day of Christ Jesus.
Who lives and reigns for ever and ever.
