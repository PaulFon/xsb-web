Option 3

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Rev 14:13

Blessed are the dead who die in the Lord. Let them rest from their labors, for their works accompany them.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, by whose mercy
the souls of the faithful find rest,
graciously grant pardon for their sins to your servants (N. and N.)
and to all who sleep in Christ,
so that, freed from all guilt,
they may have a share
in the Resurrection of your Christ.

Who lives and reigns with you.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look with favour, Lord, we pray,
on the offerings we make
for the repose of the souls of your servants,
that through these helps to human salvation
they may be granted an unfailing portion
among the multitude of your redeemed.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Communion Antiphon

Grant everlasting rest to them, O Lord, for whose memory the Body and Blood of Christ are received.

Prayer after Communion

We have received, O Lord,
the Sacrament of redemption,
and entreat your mercy
that in your compassion you may give
protection to us, the living,
and to our departed brothers and sisters
everlasting pardon.

Through Christ our Lord.
