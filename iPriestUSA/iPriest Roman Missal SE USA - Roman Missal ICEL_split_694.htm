>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who have united many nations in confessing your Name,
grant that those reborn in the font of Baptism
may be one in the faith of their hearts
and the homage of their deeds.
Through our Lord.

Or:

Attend with favour to the prayers of your people,
we ask, O Lord, and grant that the hearts of believers
may be united in your praise
and in repentance together,
so that, with division among Christians overcome,
we may hasten with joy to your eternal Kingdom
in the perfect communion of the Church.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Celebrating the memorial of our salvation,
we humbly beseech your mercy,
O Lord, that this Sacrament you give us in your compassion
may be for us the sign of unity and the bond of charity.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface: The unity of the Body of Christ, which is the Church.

Communion Antiphon | Ps 105:47

And over all these put on love, which is the bond of perfection. And let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, the peace into which you were also called in one body.

Prayer after Communion

Pour on us, O Lord, the Spirit of your love,
and in your kindness
make those you have nourished
by this one heavenly bread,
one in mind and heart.
Through Christ our Lord.
