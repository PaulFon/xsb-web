Option 6

Entrance Antiphon | Ps 83:10-11

Look, O God, on the face of your Anointed one, for one day within your courts is better than a thousand elsewhere.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Grant perpetual mercy to your departed servants,
we pray, O Lord,
that the hope and faith they had in you
may benefit them for all eternity.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

In this sacrifice, O Lord,
your Son, though innocent,
was slain for us, took away all the sins of the world;
grant, we pray, that it may set your servants (N. and N.)
free from every failing of the human condition.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Communion Antiphon | Cf. Ps 41:2-3

Like the deer that yearns for running streams, so my soul is yearning for you, my God.

Prayer after Communion

May your departed servants,
and all who sleep in Christ,
inherit eternal light, we pray, O Lord,
for while still in this life,
they received this, your holy Sacrament.

Through Christ our Lord.
