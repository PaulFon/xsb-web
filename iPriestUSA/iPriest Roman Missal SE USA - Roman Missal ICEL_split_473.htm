July 21
Saint Lawrence of Brindisi, Priest and Doctor of the Church

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who for the glory of your name and the salvation of souls
bestowed on the Priest Saint Lawrence of Brindisi
a spirit of counsel and fortitude,
grant, we pray, that in the same spirit,
we may know what must be done
and, through his intercession,
bring it to completion.

Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of Pastors: For One Pastor, or Common of Doctors of the Church, or Common of Holy Men and Women: For Religious.
