May 13
Our Lady of Fatima

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who chose the Mother of your Son
to be our Mother also,
grant us that, persevering in penance and prayer
for the salvation of the world,
we may further more effectively each day the reign of Christ.

Who lives and reigns with you
in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

From the Common of the Blessed Virgin Mary.
