19. FOR PERSECUTED CHRISTIANS

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Ps 73:20, 21, 22

Look to your covenant, O Lord, and do not forget the life of your poor ones forever.  Arise, O Lord, and defend your cause; and do not forget the voices of those who seek you!

or: | Acts 12:5

Peter thus was being kept in prison, but prayer was made without ceasing by the Church to God for him.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who in your inscrutable providence
will that the Church be united to the sufferings of your Son,
grant, we pray,
to your faithful who suffer for your Name’s sake
a spirit of patience and charity,
that they may be found true and faithful witnesses
to the promises you have made.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive, we ask, O Lord,
the prayers and sacrificial gifts we offer in humility,
and grant that those who suffer persecution
for their faithful service to you
may rejoice to be united to the sacrifice of Christ your Son
and may know that their names are written in heaven
among the company of the elect.
Through Christ our Lord.

Eucharistic Prayer for Use in Masses for Various Needs: III. Jesus, the Way to the Father.

Communion Antiphon | Mt 5:11-12

Blessed are you when they insult you and persecute you because of me, says the Lord. Rejoice and be glad, for your reward will be great in heaven.

or: | Mt 10:32

Everyone who acknowledges me before others I will acknowledge before my heavenly Father, says the Lord.

Prayer after Communion

By the power of this Sacrament, O Lord,
confirm your servants in the truth
and grant to your faithful who suffer tribulation that,
as they follow your Son in bearing their cross,
they may, in every trial,
glory in the name of Christian.
Through Christ our Lord.
