Prayer over the Offerings

Look with favour, we pray, O Lord,
on the sacrificial offerings
we present to you for the souls of your servants and,
just as you bestowed on them
the dignity of the Christian faith,
grant them also its reward.

Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon | 1 Jn 4:9

God sent his only Son into the world so that we might have life through him.

Prayer after Communion

Through these sacrificial gifts
which we have received, O Lord,
bestow on your departed servants
your great mercy and to those you endowed
with the grace of Baptism,
grant also the fullness of eternal joy.

Through Christ our Lord.
