>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Hear with favour our prayers,
which we humbly offer, O Lord,
for the salvation of the soul
of your servant, the Priest, N.,
that he, who devoted
a faithful ministry to your Name,
may rejoice in the perpetual company of your Saints.
Through our Lord.

Prayer over the Offerings

We ask your mercy, Lord,
that this sacrifice of our service, offered for the soul
of your servant, the Priest, N., may now bring pardon to him,
who devoutly offered it to you in the Church.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Prayer after Communion

Renewed by food from your heavenly table,
we humbly beseech you, O Lord,
that by the power of this sacrifice
the soul of your servant, the Priest, N.,
who faithfully ministered in your Church,
may exult for ever in your sight.
Through Christ our Lord.
