7. FOR A YOUNG PERSON

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who direct our life in all its moments,
we humbly entrust to you this servant of yours, N.,
whom we mourn as one whose life
was completed in so short a time;
grant that he (she) may flourish,
for ever young, in the happiness of your house.

Through our Lord.
