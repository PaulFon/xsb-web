Option 2

Entrance Antiphon | Cf. Is 52: 7

How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him who brings glad tidings of peace, bearing good news, announcing salvation (alleluia).

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

O God, who gave increase to your Church
through the zeal for religion
and apostolic labors of blessed N.,
grant, through his intercession,
that she may always receive
new growth in faith and in holiness.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look with favour on our supplications, O Lord,
and free us from every fault,
so that through the purifying action of your grace
we may be cleansed by the very mysteries
through which we render you service.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of Holy Pastors

Communion Antiphon | Mk 16: 15; Mt 28: 20

Go into all the world, and proclaim the Gospel. I am with you always, says the Lord (alleluia).

or: | Jn 15: 4-5

Remain in me, as I remain in you, says the Lord. Whoever remains in me and I in him bears fruit in plenty (alleluia).

Prayer after Communion

May the Sacrament we have received,
O Lord our God, nourish in us that faith
taught by the preaching of the Apostles
and kept safe by the labors of blessed N.
Through Christ our Lord.
