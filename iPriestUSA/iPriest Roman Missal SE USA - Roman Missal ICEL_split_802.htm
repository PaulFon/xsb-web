C. During Easter Time

Entrance Antiphon 1 Thess 4:14; 1 Cor 15:22

Just as Jesus died and rose again, so, through Jesus, God will bring with him those who have fallen asleep; and as in Adam all die, so also in Christ will all be brought to life, alleluia.

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Listen kindly to our prayers, O Lord:
as our faith in your Son,
raised from the dead, is deepened,
may our hope of resurrection for your servants,
also find new strength.

Through our Lord.

Or:

O God, who through the ending of present things
open up the beginning of things to come,
grant, we pray, that the soul of your servant N.
may be led by you
to attain the inheritance of eternal redemption.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Look favourably on our offerings, O Lord,
that your departed servant N.
may be taken up into glory with your Son,
in whose great mystery of love we are all united.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Communion Antiphon | Jn 11:25-26

I am the Resurrection and the Life. Whoever believes in me, even though he dies, will live, and no one who lives and believes in me will ever die, alleluia.

Prayer after Communion

Grant, we pray, O Lord, that your servant N.,
for whom we have celebrated this Paschal Sacrament,
may pass over to a dwelling place of light and peace.

Through Christ our Lord.
