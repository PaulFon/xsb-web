>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

May our prayers rise up to you, O Lord, and may the soul of N. your servant
be welcomed into eternal joy;
for as you were pleased
to create him (her) in your own image
and adopt him (her) as your own,
so command, we pray,
that he (she) may have a share
in your inheritance.

Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Mercifully receive, we pray, O Lord,
the offering we trustingly present
for the soul of your servant N.,
that through this sacrifice,
which you ordained
as the one true remedy for all,
you may grant him (her)
everlasting salvation.

Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Prayer after Communion

Renewed by the nourishment
of this sacred gift, we pray, O Lord,
that our brother (sister) N.,
freed from the bonds of death,
may rejoice to have a share
in the Resurrection of your Son.

Who lives and reigns for ever and ever.
