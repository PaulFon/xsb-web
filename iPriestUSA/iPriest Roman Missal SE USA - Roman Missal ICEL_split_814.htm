>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Free your servant N., we pray, O Lord,
from every bond of sin,
that he (she), who in this world
was found worthy to be conformed to Christ,
may be raised to the glory of the resurrection
and draw the breath of new life
among your Saints.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Grant our supplication, we pray, O Lord,
that this sacrifice may benefit your departed servant N.,
since through its offering you have granted remission
of the offenses of all the world.

Through Christ our Lord.
