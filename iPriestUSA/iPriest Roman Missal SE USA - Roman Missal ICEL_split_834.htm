4. FOR A DEACON

>> Introductory Rites and Penitential Act [Sample]

Collect

Let us pray.

And all pray in silence with the Priest for a while.

Grant, we pray, O merciful God,
a share in eternal happiness
to the soul of your servant, the Deacon, N.,
on whom you bestowed the gift
of ministering in your Church.
Through our Lord.

>> Liturgy of the Eucharist (Preparation of the Gifts)

Prayer over the Offerings

Be gracious, O Lord, to your servant,
the Deacon, N.,
for whose salvation
we offer you this sacrifice, that as in the flesh
he ministered to Christ your Son,
so may he rise up with your faithful servants
to everlasting glory.
Through Christ our Lord.

Preface for the Dead: Preface I, Preface II, Preface III, Preface IV or Preface V.

Prayer after Communion

Replenished with these sacred gifts,
we humbly entreat you, O Lord,
graciously to grant through this sacrifice
that your servant, the Deacon, N.,
whom you called to be among those who serve your Church,
once freed from the bonds of death,
may receive a share with those
who have ministered well,
and enter into your joy.
Through Christ our Lord.
