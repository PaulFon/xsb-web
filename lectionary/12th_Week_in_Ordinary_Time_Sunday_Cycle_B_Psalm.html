Give thanks to the Lord, his love is everlasting. Or: R. Alleluia. His command raised up a storm wind which tossed its waves on high. They cried to the Lord in their distress; from their straits he rescued them, He hushed the storm to a gentle breeze, and the billows of the sea were stilled. R. Give thanks to the Lord, his love is everlasting. Or: R. Alleluia. They rejoiced that they were calmed, and he brought them to their desired haven.
