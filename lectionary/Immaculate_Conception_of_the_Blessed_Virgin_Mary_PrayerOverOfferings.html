Graciously accept the saving sacrifice which
we offer you, O Lord, on the Solemnity of the Immaculate Conception of
the Blessed Virgin Mary, and grant that, as we profess her, on account
of your prevenient grace, to be untouched by any stain of sin, so,
through her intercession, we may be delivered from all our faults.
Through Christ our Lord. R. Amen.
