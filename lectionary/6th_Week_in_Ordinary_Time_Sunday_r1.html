If the man is leprous and unclean, the priest shall declare him unclean by reason of the sore on his head. The one who bears the sore of leprosy shall keep his garments rent and his head bare, and shall muffle his beard; he shall cry out, `Unclean, unclean!` As long as the sore is on him he shall declare himself unclean, since he is in fact unclean.
